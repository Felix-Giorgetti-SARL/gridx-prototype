Mobility

One concept, one passion: mobility.

The passion for classic and future mobility, its history and the excellence of its actors.
GRIDX highlights mobility in all its forms: vintage cars, modern cars, motorbikes, bicycles and electric bicycles.
Discover on site the history of traditional cars, new technologies and innovations.

A shared passion, of the past & future

GRIDX will house numerous top quality, distinctive products and high-end mobility related services.
On site, a large choice of cars, motorbikes and bicycles of all kinds can be admired: classic cars, modern cars, high-tech bicycles, motorbikes and new generations of electric cars.
The range is rounded up by a variety of supplements, from high-end fashion to small model cars; there will be racing gear, a barber, watches and even luggage.
On top of all these car-related products, GRIDX will offer complementary services such as car insurance, leasing deals and a car wash
As a special feature for passionates, GRIDX provides private storage spaces in the form of glass boxes, private boxes, garages or car club places.
A strong spirit of community will be created through our Car Club, who will be at the origin of regular events such as Rally Roads or Cars & Coffees.

Synergy creating activities

140 retail units are available to accommodate any craft or commercial activity that will constitute the heart of our concept. Customers and tenants complement each other to form a profitable community in which synergies are created in all areas and open up a new world of experience for the visitor.
In addition to the commercial and craft offer, various animated corners telling the history of vintage cars, showing new technologies and inventions, will invite visitors to a discovery tour.
A culinary and gustatory experience will be guaranteed by the restaurants and food corners, the ideal context for a stay in our design hotel or the organisation of an event in an unusual setting, whether in one of our multiple event locations or the business centre.
A 360 offer for a unique experience.

Bon-vivants

Working closely with Culinary Artists and bringing together shared values of local, organic, fresh, fair-trade and innovative. GRIDX will bring together culinary artists at all gastronomic levels in the form of gourmet restaurants, food-hall, modular spaces and food tech incubators; to give imaginative young start-up's the opportunity to bring their products to a large and interested market. GRIDX's comprehensive culinary variety will create the go-to destination for foodies far and wide.

Out of the ordinary

In todays world building trust, values and communities around brands is essential for success. It's no-longer just about the product, the product must represent the values of the consumer. GRIDX will be working closely with retailers and restaurateurs to create a unique offer to display, educate and commercialise their exceptional products. Helping brands to create trust and build communities around our shared values to offer a unique and inclusive experience for the consumer will help create transformative and positive brand perceptions for all tenants.
